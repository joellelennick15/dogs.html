Dogs are The Best Pets

Dogs are the best pets because they will always be there for you. They cheer you up when you're down and want to please their owners.

Perfect Pets

Dogs can be the perfect pet for almost anyone. They can be big or small, which can help with having a big or small living space. There can be service dogs that help a person in need of assistance in their daily life. The different breeds of dogs can have very different impacts on what you want in a dog. (i.e. If you want a quiet dog, then don't get a hound. If you want a calm dog, then don't get a border collie.)
